1. ## The Best Of the Best - Chili Octopus

<div class="photo-grid-2">
  <img src="/octopus.png" alt="На лагуната Балос">
  <img src="/octopus2.jpg" alt="Плаж Балос">
</div>
In Ukulhas you can eat as many sea food as you wish. But the best thing we tried was, without any doubt, the chili octopus. Either the whole octopus(which is a little bit tough) or in pieces, it is slightly spicy and very very delicious. It is only meat, so it contains a lot of protein. What is greater than that?

You can eat spicy octopus in almost every restaurant in Ukulhas. The ones we prefer are [Paguro](https://www.paguromaldives.com/) and [Olhumathi](https://share.google/vdNYOyJJe2wIPOUKO). The prices are good,  the octopus is aroun 10$.

2. ## Coconuts 
![Coconut](/coconuts.avif)
Not sure if I like them because they look fancy and give you the feeling of an exotic place..but if they are matured, they are so tasty and are good choice for very warm days. 

3. ## Passion Fruit Juice
![Passion Fruit](/passionfruit.avif)
I haven't tried something so fresh and tasty, the best fresh juice ever! You could drink it almost everywhere I guess. This photo is from [Retro Royal](https://share.google/7hNMJ0edUIoz9B9vl)

4. ## Maldivian Style Pizza
![Pizza](/pizza.avif)
What is the special thing in this kind of pizza? It consists a lot of sea food, the dough is light and it has a lot of products on it. I don't know anyone who is not a pizza lover, do you?

Where to eat it - again in [Olhumathi](https://share.google/vdNYOyJJe2wIPOUKO)

And a couple of interesting foods, drinks and places to try

- Maldivian style breakfast 
It was included in the hotel's reservation and there were 3 options - Maldivian style breakfast, American style breakfast, consisting french toasts and sausages and something in between; I always want to try something new and I really enjoyed the Maldivian breakfast - it includes something like pancakes but thinner, an omelette and a kind of a sauce with tomatoes and a lot of onions
![breakfast](/breakfast.avif)

- Shrimps - definitely not for everyone but an interesting choice when serve in that way
![shrimps](/shrimps.avif)

- Sweets - I cannot say there are typical fruits in Maldives expect the smaller bananas but when we ordered a dessert in [Olhumathi](https://share.google/vdNYOyJJe2wIPOUKO) we tried some foods, a sweet salami and something like candied candy(the red one)
![sweets](/sweets.avif)

- And here is our improvised Christmas Eve dinner - some of foods we brought with ourselves, some took from the nearest shop and some we ordered at the hotel.
![christmaseve](/christmaseve.avif)

- And the best Christmas dinner - at [Celeste Restaurant](https://www.google.com/maps/place//data=!4m2!3m1!1s0x3b4057c59f7a3a55:0xd59520f0cd8b8dfb?source=g.page.share) - we were full and there were everything you could imagine; actually I liked the chocolate mouse the most 
![christmaseve](/christmas.avif)

And the last fact needed to be mentioned - maybe it is well known nut there is NO alcohol in Maldives. There is only non-alcoholic ginger beer. But isn't it funny - the weather and the ocean make you drunk enough.
